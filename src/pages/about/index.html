FAQs

Have questions?

Here are some quick answers to the most common questions about our services, pricing, and how we work

What’s included in the branding design service?

Our branding service covers everything from logo design and color palette to typography and visual direction. We also provide brand guidelines so you can stay consistent across platforms.

How long does a typical website project take?

Timelines vary based on the scope, but most projects take between 1 to 4 weeks. One-page sites are usually done in a week, while full multi-page builds may take a bit longer.

Can I request custom features for Framer?

Absolutely! We can implement custom animations, interactions, and even integrate third-party tools, just let us know what you need.

Are there any hidden costs beyond the listed pricing?

Nope, what you see is what you get. If you request extra features outside of the initial scope, we’ll always discuss and agree on any added costs beforehand.

Do I need to prepare anything before we start?

Yes, but we’ll guide you! Ideally, have your content (copy, images, links) ready. If you need help with content, we can support or connect you with a copywriting service. We also provide brand guidelines so you can stay consistent across platforms.

What if I need changes after the project is done?

Each package includes a round of revisions, and our Studio+ plan includes 30 days of post-launch support. Additional updates can be handled on a flexible, hourly basis.
